Our team is dedicated to providing
outstanding services in voiceover and translation

The Haitian Creole Voices and More team is a dynamic group of skilled professionals dedicated to bridging linguistic and cultural gaps through exceptional translation, interpretation, and voiceover services. Fluent in Haitian Creole, English, and French, our team brings decades of combined expertise in linguistics, technology, and creative communication.

Each member is committed to delivering culturally nuanced and accurate solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. From translating complex legal documents to creating engaging e-learning voiceovers and providing seamless live interpretation, our team ensures the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

At Haitian Creole Voices and More, we are passionate about empowering individuals and organizations to connect meaningfully across languages and cultures, fostering understanding and collaboration in every project we undertake.
